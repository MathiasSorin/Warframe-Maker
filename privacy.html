Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Warframe Mod Maker is a free browser-based tool that allows users to create custom Warframe mods and arcane card images. This page explains how data is handled when you use the site.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All card creation happens entirely in your browser, no information you enter (mod names, effects, uploaded images) is ever sent to a server.

Cookies and Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

- Google AdSense for advertising
- Google Fonts for typography
- html2canvas (loaded via CDN) for card image generation
