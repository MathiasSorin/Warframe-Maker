How to

Mod Card

  1. Select a Rarity to set the card's border.
  2. Enter the Drain cost and choose a Polarity.
  3. Fill in the Mod Name, Effect, and Item Type.
  4. Set the Rank (0-10) to display rank slots.
  5. Upload a custom Card Art image (use the in-game Captura mode to take amazing screenshots that fit your mod idea!)
  6. Click Download Card to save your card as a PNG.
  7. Share it with the community on r/Warframe or the official Warframe forums.

Arcane Card

  1. Enter the Arcane Name and set the Rank (0-5).
  2. Fill in the Item Type (Warframe, Primary, Secondary, etc.).
  3. Write the Effect description.
  4. Choose an Arcane Art from the dropdown.
  5. Click Download Card to save your card as a PNG.
  6. Share it with the community on r/Warframe or the official Warframe forums.
