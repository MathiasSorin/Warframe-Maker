About

Warframe Mod Maker

Hello fellow Tenno!

Firetwice, LR6 founder here!

I have been playing Warframe for 13 years. This game has given me so much, including one of my best friends, who I met while farming for Frost prime in 2013. This tool is my way of giving a little something back to a game and a community that has meant so much to me.

The idea came from my love of Hearthstone and its custom card scene. Warframe's modding system has an enormous design space potential waiting to be explored. So I built this to give the community a way to bring those ideas to life.

Unleash your creativity, have fun!

I genuinely cannot wait to see what you guys come up with!
